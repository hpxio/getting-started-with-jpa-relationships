Course - ID, Name 
Review - ID, Rating, Comment

Course can have N reviews
But
One/N review can be related to one course
SO, any how one course can have N reviews - From Course perspective, it is One to Many relation
Conversely, N reviews can be related to one course, forming Many to One Relation
